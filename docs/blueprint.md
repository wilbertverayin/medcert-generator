# **App Name**: MedCert Lite

## Core Features:

- Patient Details Form: Form to capture patient details: Patient Name, Age, Sex, Date of Consultation, Diagnosis, Duration of Leave, Remarks.
- Doctor Details: Form will include Doctor Name and PRC Number, pre-filled from profile. Clinic name and logo shown as well
- Unique ID Generator: Generates a 6-digit unique certificate ID.
- Medical Extraction Tool: AI powered "tool" to extract medical entity and diagnosis from the remarks that were provided by the doctor.
- QR Code Generation: Generates QR code linking to verification page based on certificate ID.
- Certificate Verification Page: Displays read-only certificate data using unique certificate ID.
- Profile Settings: Doctors can upload digital signatures, clinic logos and maintain profile information

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: dark blue (#00008B) for a calm and trustworthy feel, relating to health and medicine.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF), almost white, to ensure readability and a clean interface.
- Accent color: A Light blue (#ADD8E6) for interactive elements and highlights, signifying health and recovery.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', sans-serif.
- Headline text: 'Literata', serif. Chosen for a classic, readable and reliable feel in short snippets of text.
- Use clean and professional line icons relevant to healthcare and user actions.
- Simple and intuitive layout with a focus on ease of use and quick access to features.
